About me

Hi, I'm Callum a programmer from the North West of England.

Ever since I was little, I have always had an interest in video games from watching my dad play on the original Xbox to recieving my first Playstation 2 console. Secondary school was when I found out about careers in the games industry and since then it has been my ambition to enter this industry. I have studied computing in college, at HND level, achieved a Bachelors of Science degree in computer games programming and a Master of Science in computing this year. My main area of focus throughout my study has been in relation to the Unity games engine.

However, I also have experince in other software packages including Blender for 3D modelling, Unreal Engine, Photoshop and Gimp for Texture creation and Adobe XD for UI design.
